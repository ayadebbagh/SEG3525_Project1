About us

Verdant Blossoms is Ottawa's premier women-owned floral design studio. We're passionate about elevating your world with modern, unforgettable floral arrangements.

Our team of talented women is dedicated to crafting bespoke experiences for every occasion. We source the freshest, most beautiful blooms and use them to design arrangements that tell a story and capture your vision.

Whether you're seeking a statement piece for your home, a breathtaking bouquet for a loved one, or exquisite florals for your wedding or special event, we're here to bring your floral dreams to life.
